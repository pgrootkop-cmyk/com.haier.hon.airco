Control your Haier air conditioners with Homey via the hOn cloud API.

**Usage**
- Install this app on your Homey
- Add a device and select Haier hOn > Air Conditioner
- Log in with your hOn account credentials
- Select your air conditioner from the list

Pairing must be done from the Homey desktop or web interface. The mobile app does not support the OAuth2 login screen.

We recommend using a separate hOn account for Homey to avoid potential session conflicts.

**Supported Features**
- HVAC Modes: Auto, Cool, Heat, Dry, Fan Only, Anti-Freeze
- Fan Speed: Auto, Low, Medium, High
- Swing: Off, Vertical, Horizontal, Both
- Eco Pilot: Off, Avoid Me, Follow Me
- Toggles: Silent, Rapid, Sleep, Eco, Health, Screen Display, Beep
- Sensors: Indoor Temperature, Outdoor Temperature
- Flow Cards: Triggers, Conditions, and Actions for all features

**Supported Brands**
Works with any air conditioner controllable via the hOn app, including Haier, Candy, and Hoover.
